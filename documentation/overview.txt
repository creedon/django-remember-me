===================
Django remember_me
===================


*this document is in progress*

This is a replacement for Django_'s `django.contrib.auth.views.login`_, that
adds a Remember Me feature to the login form.

.. _views.login documentation: http://docs.djangoproject.com/en/dev/topics/auth/#django.contrib.auth.login
